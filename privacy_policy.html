Privacy Policy for PlaySoft

Last updated: January 2025
Effective Date: January 2025

1. Introduction

PlaySoft ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("PlaySoft" or "App"). By downloading, installing, or using PlaySoft, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

PlaySoft is a multimedia entertainment application that allows users to play local audio and video files, enjoy various games, and access media content on their Android devices.

Key Points:

  • We do not collect personal information like names, emails, or phone numbers
  • We only access local media files for playback purposes
  • We use third-party advertising services with user consent
  • We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations
  • Users have full control over their data and consent preferences

2. Information We Collect

A. Personal Data

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or contact details.

B. Usage Data & Device Information

We may collect non-personal information about your device and how you use the App, including:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • Usage statistics (e.g., screens viewed, features used, ad interactions)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Approximate location (inferred from IP address for GDPR region detection)

C. Media Access

The App requests permission to access your device's media files (audio and video) solely to display and play your local media content. We do not upload, transmit, or share your media files with any third parties. All media files remain on your device.

D. Game Data

Game scores, levels, and progress are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

3. Permissions Used

The App requests the following permissions for functionality:

  • READ_MEDIA_AUDIO / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO (Android 13+): To access and play your local audio and video files
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below): To access and play your local media files
  • MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS: To enable equalizer and audio enhancements
  • READ_PHONE_STATE: To detect your region for GDPR compliance purposes only
  • INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To display ads and check network connectivity
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE & FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK: To play media in the background
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE & CHANGE_WIFI_STATE: For Google Cast functionality

Note: We only use these permissions for the stated purposes. You can revoke permissions at any time through your device settings, though this may limit app functionality.

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. Google may collect and use device identifiers, IP address, approximate location, app interactions, diagnostics, and other information as described in their privacy policy.

Google's use of data:

  • For ad personalization, analytics, and fraud prevention
  • May use cookies and similar technologies
  • All data sent to Google is encrypted in transit
  • Google may share data with their advertising partners

You can learn more about Google's data practices here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Privacy
  • Google Advertising ID

Opt-out options:

  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
  • Reset your Advertising ID in your device settings
  • Withdraw consent for ad personalization in the App settings

B. GDPR and Consent

  • If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), the App will ask for your consent before showing personalized ads or collecting data for advertising purposes
  • The App automatically detects if you are in a GDPR region
  • You can change your consent at any time in the App's settings
  • If you withdraw consent, no further data will be collected or shared for advertising purposes, but previously collected data may still be retained by Google according to their policies

5. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the App's features and user experience
  • Display ads (with your consent, where required)
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends
  • Fix bugs and improve performance
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information.
  • We may share non-personal, aggregated information with analytics providers or ad networks
  • Your data may be shared with Google AdMob as described above
  • All data transmitted off your device is encrypted in transit
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Secure API communications
  • Local data storage on your device

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Data Retention

  • Non-personal usage data may be retained for up to 24 months for analytics and improvement purposes
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data are retained for up to 12 months
  • Advertising data is retained according to Google's policies (typically 24 months)
  • Local app data (game scores, preferences) is stored on your device until you uninstall the app

Data Deletion

  • Users can withdraw consent for data collection at any time in the App's settings
  • To request deletion of data held by third parties, please contact them directly:
    • Google: Google Privacy Help
  • App uninstallation will stop all future data collection
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance or security purposes

Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data

CCPA (California)

Other Jurisdictions

We respect privacy laws in all jurisdictions where our App is available. Additional rights may apply based on your location.
